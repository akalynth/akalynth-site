The House Plots

Preview buying a plot in Azura at a fixed price, paid in in-game gold — separate from the shop's premium Azura coins. Own one? You can resell it to other players by listing it for gold.

Housing is in the city of Azura. Rookguard — the training zone — has no houses.

⚠ House purchases and resale are previewed here using in-game gold. Auctions are planned and will not become available until the server-side receipt and verifier lane is implemented. This page is a local preview: it does not prove ownership, spend real currency, or affect live game state.

Auctions — planned

In a future update, scarce plots may be allocated by gold auctions instead of fixed price, and owners will be able to auction a house as well as resell it at a set price. This is not available yet — it goes live only once the server-side auction receipts, escrow/refund handling, settlement receipts, and verifier checks exist. Nothing on this page places a live bid or runs an auction.
