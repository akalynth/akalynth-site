The Route

Rookguard — the Threshold

Rookguard is the stone antechamber every newcomer crosses before the world will admit them. Deliberately small and quiet, it is where each guest first wakes and warms up on the core of Akalynth: how to move, how to speak, and how to answer the guardian Tem. Beside the corridor stand two old stones — the Runestone Table and the Legend Stone. When a traveller has shown a living hand is at the keyboard, the gate to High City opens.

High City — the First City

Beyond the gate, the world widens. After the closed quiet of the keep, walls give way to open ground and the city receives newcomers at its center. High City is intentionally unfinished — its empty plots and silent hall are not gaps to apologize for, but room left for players to become the reason a hall has a guild and a plot has a name.
